Its research involves many fields such as perception, cognition, emotion, thinking, personality, behavioral habits, interpersonal relationship, social relationship, artificial intelligence, IQ, personality and so on. It is also related to many fields of daily life -- family, education, health and society. 1) Mental process refers to the dynamic process of a person's mental phenomena. It includes the process of cognition, emotion and will, reflecting the common aspect of normal individual psychological phenomena. The cognitive process is the psychological process of receiving, encoding, storing, extracting and applying cognitive information in practice. It mainly includes sensory perception, thinking, memory and so on. The emotional process is the experience of an individual's attitude towards things in practice. Volitional process is a psychological process in which individuals consciously determine goals, adjust and control their own actions according to the goals, and overcome difficulties to achieve predetermined goals. The above three processes are not isolated from each other, but interrelated and interacting to form three different aspects of individual organic and unified psychological process. (2) Individual mind is the sum total of various relatively stable psychological phenomena formed by a person in social life practice. Including personality tendency, personality characteristic and personality regulation and so on, reflect the individual side of human psychological phenomenon. Personality tendency is the dynamic system that impels people to carry out activities. It reflects people's tendency and pursuit of the world around them. It mainly includes needs, motives, interests, ideals, beliefs, values and world views. Personality characteristics are the essential and stable psychological characteristics that are often displayed on an individual. It mainly includes temperament, character and ability.
